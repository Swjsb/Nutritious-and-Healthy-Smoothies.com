Fruits & Yogurt Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of frozen strawberry or berries like blueberry.
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt or any flavored yogurt
  • 1 cup of plain pineapple juice
  • 1 banana,peeled & sliced
Method
  • Combine all the ingredients & blend them till smooth.

Mango Smoothie

ingredients

  • 2 fresh mangoes or frozen slicies of mangos.
  • 1 frozen banana, can use unfrozen if you prefer using frozen mangoes.
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt
Method
  • Add all the ingredients to the blender and blend until Creamy.

Apple Date Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 4-6 dates,de-seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 cup roughly chopped apple, peeled and deseeded
  • 1 cup of cold or chill milk
  • Pinch of Cinnamon
Method
  • Blend all the ingredients until smooth and frothy.

Banana Blend Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2-3 frozen banan, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup of plain or flavored yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of chill milk
  • 1/3 tsp of vanilla essence
  • 2 tsp of honey
  • Pinch of Cinnamon(optional)
Method
  • Blend all the ingredients in the blender until smooth.

StrawBerry Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2-3 cups of fresh strawberries, cut into fine pieces
  • 1 banana, quartered and frozen
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1tsp of honey
Method
  • Add all the ingredients to the blender and blend it until smooth.

Musk Melon Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 full of musk melon, peeled,de-seeded and chopped into fine pieces
  • 1/2 cup of either flavored or plain
  • 1tsp of honey
  • ice-cubes as per your requirement
Method
  • Blend all the ingredients for 15-20 seconds,till smooth

Berry Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 3/4 cup of mixed berries
  • 1/3 cup of black grapes
  • 1tsp of chia seeds
  • 1tsp of almond or peanut butter
  • 1tsp of honey
  • 1/2 cup of either almond milk or any milk of your choice
  • Toppings of your choice (i usually pefer dates as my toppings)
Method
  • Place all the ingredients into the blender and blend until thick and smooth.Pour into bowl.
  • Top it with chia seeds,strawberries(berries of your choice),banana, dates,almond,cashew.

Chill with Smoothie while you relax.
